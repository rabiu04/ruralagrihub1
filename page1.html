To make available rightly sourced and safe farm produce to consumers through effective use of technology and support smallholders to have access to secured market by adopting sustainable practices and standards in the production process.

To be the lead promoter of agribusiness in Africa and support 1 million smallholder farmers annually to cut down food waste through secured markets and fairer prices.

Our customers: The needs of both our consumers and farmers are our utmost priority. We serve and support them in many ways.

Learning and Continuous Improvement: Our employees thrive in an atmosphere which supports continuous learning development and personal growth.

Innovation and creativity: We believe in an innovative, entrepreneurial environment that empowers great ideas. We constantly strive to find new and better ways to meet our customers’ needs and exceed their expectations.

PROFIT The primary responsibility of our business, along with the management and individual employees, is to make a profit. Profit is absolutely essential for the very existence of our business on a long-term basis.

Environmental sensitivity: We recognize our responsibilities to the communities in which we operate. We are proactive in dedicating resources to build a better quality of life, operate in an ethical and environmentally sensitive manner and live by our values.

Our Team: Our team is our most valuable asset. We recognize employees focus on making Rural AgriHub their first choice for work. Our team consists of professional managers with experience. We have a “One Team” attitude and approach to work, and we engage equally and efficiently to deliver the best project and services to our clients. Although we have a pragmatic drive for action, our approach is also friendly and down to earth, as we collaborate with both international organisations and the local communities to achieve the goals of our projects. We care about the relationships we build with our clients and members of the community. Just as much as we care about getting things done. We believe in respecting diversity and in encouraging teamwork, involvement, development and empowerment of all employees.

Rural Agrihub is a social enterprise integrating sellers from rural areas with buyers of fresh fruits, vegetables and agricultural produce at the urban areas.

We aggregates demand and delivers a wide range of both organic and non organic produce directly from farms to stores of B2B customers retailers, wholesalers, food processors, restaurants, exporters and institutional kitchens. We support local producers, local communities and small farmers on crop planning in alignment with marketplaces, upgrading farm side infrastructure and transition to sustainable practices as well incorporating sustainability standards through farmer trainings.

We leverages on technology to assure smooth order execution from farms to stores while organizing quality checks, distribution and traceability.
